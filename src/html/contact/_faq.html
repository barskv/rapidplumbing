FAQ's

  • How do I know if I have a plumbing emergency?

    Plumbing emergencies can include burst pipes, sewage backups, or sudden loss of water supply. If you're experiencing any of these issues, it's essential to call us immediately at our 24/7 emergency hotline. We'll dispatch a team promptly to address the situation and prevent further damage to your property

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods for your convenience, including cash, credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), and electronic bank transfers. Our technicians can provide you with a detailed invoice and process your payment securely on-site after the completion of the service

  • How soon can I expect a technician to arrive for my scheduled appointment?

    We understand the importance of prompt service, and we strive to schedule appointments at your earliest convenience. Typically, our technicians arrive within a two-hour window of your scheduled appointment time. If there are any delays or changes to the schedule, we'll notify you promptly to keep you informed

  • Do you offer warranties on your plumbing services?

    Yes, we stand behind the quality of our workmanship and offer warranties on parts and labor for our plumbing services. The specific warranty terms may vary depending on the type of service provided and the products used. We're committed to ensuring your satisfaction and will address any issues covered under warranty promptly and efficiently.

  • Can I attempt to fix a plumbing problem myself before calling for professional help?

    While there are some minor plumbing issues you may be able to address on your own, such as a clogged drain or a leaky faucet, it's essential to exercise caution. Attempting complex repairs without the proper knowledge and tools can lead to further damage and costly repairs down the line. If you're unsure or the problem persists after your attempts, it's best to call us for professional assistance. We have the expertise and equipment to diagnose and resolve plumbing issues efficiently, saving you time and hassle in the long run
